# TechCorp Employee Handbook

> Version 3.2 | Last updated: 2026-01-15

---

## 1. Code of Conduct

All employees must adhere to TechCorp's core values: **Innovation, Integrity, Inclusion, Impact**. Harassment of any kind is strictly prohibited. Violations should be reported to HR or via the anonymous ethics hotline at ethics@techcorp.com.

## 2. Remote Work Policy

TechCorp operates a remote-first environment. Employees may work from anywhere within their country of employment, provided they maintain a minimum of 4 hours overlap with their team's core hours (9 AM – 1 PM ET).

- Home office stipend: $2,000/year for equipment
- Internet reimbursement: $100/month
- Coworking space budget: $300/month

## 3. Vacation and Time Off

- **Annual leave**: 25 days per year, prorated for partial years
- **Sick leave**: 15 days per year
- **Parental leave**: 20 weeks fully paid, regardless of gender
- **Sabbatical**: 4 weeks paid every 4 years of service

Vacation requests must be submitted via Workday at least 2 weeks in advance. Emergency leave should be communicated to your manager directly.

## 4. Equipment and Security

- Company-issued laptop: MacBook Pro (replaced every 3 years)
- All devices must have disk encryption enabled
- Password manager (1Password) is mandatory
- MFA is required for all corporate accounts
- Security incidents must be reported to security@techcorp.com within 1 hour

## 5. Performance Review Cycle

Reviews happen twice per year:
- **Mid-year review** (June): Check-in, goal adjustment
- **Annual review** (December): Full performance evaluation, promotion consideration

Managers must submit written reviews 1 week before the review meeting. Employees receive a self-assessment template 2 weeks prior.

## 6. Learning and Development

- Annual learning budget: $5,000 per employee
- Conference attendance: up to 2 conferences per year, fully covered
- Internal tech talks every Friday (recorded for async viewing)
- Mentorship program: 1 hour/week with an assigned mentor during first 6 months
